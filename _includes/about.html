About

Hello! My name is Alvaro Cerezo and I am an architect and urban planner specializing in Data Science and GIS technologies. I hold a Msc in Architecture & Urban planning from Universidad Europea de Madrid and a Master in City & Technology from the Institute of Advanced Architecture of Catalonia.

My research interests are focusing on the intersection of Urban Planning and Internet of Things. Currently, I am focused on building location intelligence applications and interactive maps to provide multiple stakeholders with new insights on the urban context.

I consider myself as a very dedicated and hard-worker professional that have had the great opportunity to be part of several multidisciplinary and multicultural teams both in my working and education career.
